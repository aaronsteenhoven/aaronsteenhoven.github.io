ABOUT ME

Some people believe that cats have 9 lives...sometimes it feels like I've had that many careers. Originally from Chicago, I grew up the son of a preacher. Early on in my career, I pursued church ministry...working as a Youth Pastor, Worship Pastor, and then later as the lead Pastor of a church plant in the Chicago area. After moving to Nashville, I transitioned to pursuing music full time. I spent 7 years as a songwriter/producer, writing over 700 songs and producing approximately 200 tracks.

While I was working in ministry and pursuing a career as a songwriter, my wife and I also opened an online retail operation that had immediate success. In 10+ years we were able to push close to $2 mil in sales. We opened our first physical storefront in Hendersonville, TN in 2014. After the birth of our daughter, we sold the physical portion of our store in March of 2017 for six figures. The experience that I gained in business development and finance as a result of our company propelled me to analyst roles in the corporate realm.

I started my journey into the tech industry over a year ago, when I was managing the company database at Concord Music Group in Nashville. Working with Microsoft SQL Server was an interesting experience that got me interested in learning more about coding and web development.In March of 2019, I made the decision to invest in my future by attending a 12 week immersive coding bootcamp at Eleven Fifty Academy in Fishers, IN. They helped me build on my SQL background with front end experience, and helped me unleash my potential as a developer.
